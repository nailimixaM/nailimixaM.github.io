## About ##

I'm a PhD student working on Bayesian deep learning and uncertainty quantification applied to fluid mechanics settings, supervised by Professor Matthew Juniper.

I completed my BA and MEng in engineering at Fitzwilliam College, Cambridge in 2018. In October 2018 I started my PhD at Pembroke College, Cambridge.
